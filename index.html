About Me

I am an incoming Ph.D. student at the School of Software, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Bin Wang, where I will focus on Computer Graphics and 3D Scene Reconstruction starting in Fall 2025. My current research interests include 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS), dynamic scene reconstruction, and photorealistic video generation.

I am currently completing my Master's degree at Northeastern University, advised by Prof. Dongyue Chen. My previous work has centered around 3D Vision and Machine Learning, with publications in top-tier conferences such as CVPR, ICCV, and AAAI.
